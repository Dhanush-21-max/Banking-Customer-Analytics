\#Banking Customer Analytics



\##Project Overview



This project analyzes banking customer data to understand customer demographics, financial behavior, banking relationships, deposits, loans, income, and risk profile. The analysis helps generate business insights that support better decision-making.



\## Business Problem



Banks collect large amounts of customer data but often struggle to convert it into meaningful business insights. This project analyzes customer information to identify patterns and support data-driven decisions.



\## Objectives



\- Understand customer demographics.

\- Analyze banking relationships.

\- Study deposits and loans.

\- Analyze customer income.

\- Perform risk analysis.

\- Generate business recommendations.



\## Dataset Information



\- Dataset Name: Banking Data
